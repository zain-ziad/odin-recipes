Chicken Tikka

Product Description

Shan Chicken Tikka Mix helps you prepare mouth-watering, flavorsome and juicy charbroiled chicken to satiate your barbecue craving.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Lemon Juice
  • Oil
  • Recipe Mix Packet

Steps

  1. Mix together Shan Chicken Tikka Mix, lemon juice and 2 tablespoons oil. Apply on chicken pieces and marinate for 3 hours +.
  2. Put each portion of chicken on skewer and place it on low heat of charcoal / gas grill.
  3. Grill evenly on each side until meat is tender and done. Apply butter before removing from heat. Serving Suggestions: Serve hot Chicken Tikka with fresh green salad, sliced onion, tomatoes and Shan Tamarind / BBQ / Green Chutney.
